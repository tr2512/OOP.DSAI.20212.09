A nutrition transportation system of the cell which has many important functions such as protein folding and lipid formation. It's composed by interconnected network of flattened, membrane-enclosed sacs, called cisterna (xoang) or tubular structures. The endoplasmic reticulum 's membrane are continous with the outer of nucleus membrane. There are 2 types of endoplasmic reticulum: rough ER (with the ribosomes attached to) and smooth ER (without ribosomes attached to)
